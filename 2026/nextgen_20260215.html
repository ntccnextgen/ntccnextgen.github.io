God’s Word Renews Our Minds

  • Romans 12:2

    • Two words:

      • Conformed → passive shaping from outside
        • Transformed → inner renewal by God

    • The Greek word for transformed is metamorphoō

    • Same root as “metamorphosis.”

    • It’s not cosmetic change.

    • It’s inner restructuring.

  • God’s Word doesn’t just adjust behavior.

  • It rewires how we think.

Growth Happens Quietly Over Time

  • Verse Focus: Psalm 1:3

    • “Like a tree planted by streams of water…”

  • Roots grow unseen

  • Fruit comes later

  • Stability comes first

  • Phone charging daily vs. once a week

  • Deep roots surviving drought

  • Loud growth is rare.

  • Lasting growth is slow.
